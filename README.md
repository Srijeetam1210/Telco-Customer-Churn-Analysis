**Telco Customer Churn Analysis**

**Overview :**
This project analyzes customer churn behavior using the Telco Customer Churn dataset from Kaggle. The goal is to identify the major reasons behind customer attrition and provide actionable business recommendations to improve customer retention.

**📌 Problem Statement:**
Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges in the telecom industry.
This project aims to answer:
Which customers are most likely to churn?
What factors contribute most to churn?
How can the business reduce customer attrition?

**📈 Key Insights:**
Overall churn rate: 26.5%
Customers with 1–2 months tenure churn the most
Month-to-month contracts have the highest churn risk
Senior citizens are more likely to churn
Customers using electronic check payments show higher churn
Customers without: Online Security, Tech Support, Device Protection are more likely to leave

**💡 Business Recommendations:**
Improve onboarding for new customers
Encourage annual or long-term contracts
Provide dedicated support for senior citizens
Bundle security & support services
Promote auto-pay payment methods
